What We Offer

Professional medical care across a wide range of health needs

🩺

General Consultations

See an experienced GP for any health concern. Fast, thorough, and affordable consultations for the whole family.

  • Adult & child consultations
  • Flu, coughs, infections & fevers
  • Allergies & skin conditions
  • Stomach & digestive problems
  • Headaches & body pains
  • Referral letters to specialists
❤️

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care and monitoring for long-term conditions. We help you manage your health every step of the way.

  • Diabetes (sugar) management
  • High blood pressure / hypertension
  • Cholesterol management
  • Asthma & respiratory conditions
  • HIV/AIDS care & ART management
  • Epilepsy & thyroid disorders
👩

Women's Health

Dedicated care for women at every stage of life, from family planning to pregnancy and beyond.

  • Pregnancy testing & antenatal care
  • Family planning & contraception
  • Pap smear screening
  • Breast examinations
  • Menstrual & hormonal concerns
  • UTI & vaginal infections
👶

Children's Health

Caring for your little ones from birth through childhood with gentle, professional paediatric services.

  • Baby immunisations & vaccinations
  • Growth monitoring
  • Childhood illnesses & infections
  • Ear, nose & throat problems
  • Skin rashes & allergies
  • School health forms
🔬

Minor Surgery & Procedures

Quick in-office procedures performed by our experienced medical team. No hospital visit needed.

  • Wound stitching & treatment
  • Abscess drainage
  • Ingrown toenail removal
  • Ear syringing (wax removal)
  • Burn treatment & dressings
  • Foreign body removal
📋

Medical Certificates & Workplace Health

Fast, professional documentation for your employer, school, or legal requirements.

  • Sick notes / medical certificates
  • Medical fitness certificates
  • Pre-employment medicals
  • Driver's medical (PrDP)
  • Occupational health assessments
  • Insurance & disability forms
🧪

Health Screenings & Tests

Preventive health checks and diagnostic testing to catch problems early and keep you healthy.

  • Blood tests (sugar, cholesterol, full blood count)
  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • STI / STD testing
  • Pregnancy tests
  • Urine analysis
  • Flu vaccinations (seasonal)
🫁

TB Screening & Treatment

Complete tuberculosis care from screening to treatment completion. Confidential and compassionate support.

  • TB screening & testing
  • Sputum collection
  • DOTS treatment programme
  • Treatment monitoring
  • Contact tracing support
  • TB/HIV co-management
🏃

Sports & Injury Care

Prompt treatment for injuries, sprains, and musculoskeletal problems to get you back on your feet.

  • Sports injuries assessment
  • Sprains & strains
  • Back & neck pain
  • Joint pain & arthritis
  • Work-related injuries
  • Physiotherapy referrals
