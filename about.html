Our Story

Your Trusted Family Doctor in Piet Retief

Dr. Prefa Medical Centre is a patient-centred general practice located at Kempville Shopping Centre, Piet Retief. We provide comprehensive primary healthcare services to individuals and families across the community.

Led by Dr. V. Prefa, our practice brings together over 17 years of clinical experience spanning multiple healthcare settings across Nigeria, Lesotho, and South Africa. From routine consultations and chronic disease management to minor procedures and wellness services, we are committed to delivering professional, compassionate care.

We believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone. That's why we accept most major medical aids and welcome cash patients at affordable rates.
