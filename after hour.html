When to Call

Conditions We Treat After Hours

Our after-hours service covers a wide range of urgent medical needs.

🤒

Fever & Infections

High fevers, flu symptoms, ear infections, throat infections, and UTIs

🤕

Pain & Injuries

Severe headaches, back pain, sprains, minor wounds, and burns

😷

Respiratory Issues

Asthma attacks, breathing difficulty, croup, chest infections

🤢

Stomach & GI

Severe nausea, vomiting, diarrhoea, dehydration, abdominal pain

🩹

Cuts & Wounds

Lacerations requiring stitches, wound cleaning, bite injuries

⚡

Allergic Reactions

Hives, allergic swelling, insect bite reactions, medication reactions

⚠️ Life-Threatening Emergencies

For severe chest pain, stroke symptoms, heavy uncontrolled bleeding, loss of consciousness, or other life-threatening emergencies, please call 10177 (ambulance) or go directly to your nearest hospital emergency department.
